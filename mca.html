Mouloudia Club d'Alger

Club du Doyen | Forever Green and Red

Born in 1921, Mouloudia Club d'Alger stands as the first Algerian Muslim club a symbol of identity, resistance, and pride. The fire of the Chnaoua burns far beyond the stadium, carried in every heart that bleeds red and green.

The soul of the MCA lives in the heart of Algiers from "Ram Jaw Lawlad Casbah Bab El Oued". And me? I carry all of that in my blood. This page reflects who I am a devoted lover of MCA, shaped by its history, its pride, and the unbreakable spirit of the Doyen.
